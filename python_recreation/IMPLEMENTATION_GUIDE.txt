CORE RULES
- Remove all boss code. No boss arenas, boss drops, boss counters, or boss exceptions.
- Default loadout: ranged pistol/smg/shotgun/rifle/sniper/rocket, melee knife, misc grenade + medkit.
- Movement is intentionally nerfed: low base speed, weaker dash/roll bursts, hard speed cap near 22 m/s.
- Keybinds load from keybinds.json and must be re-read/applied immediately after settings changes.

SPAWNING
- Every 5 seconds spawn 5 enemies. SON doubles the amount.
- Every 666m add +6 enemies per 5s wave.
- Caps: DUNCE 7 increases, ALRIGHT 15, SON 40.
- Every 5th increase display exactly: (THE TIDE RISES)
- Scale spawn timer by current player velocity so sprinting never creates empty no-enemy stretches.

ENEMIES
- All enemies get double jump and short dash cooldown.
- Necromancer: ALRIGHT only after 2000m, black wizard, 200 HP, summons Minions.
- Minion: 30 HP, 20 touch damage.
- THE BRON: SON only after 1700m, black basketball player, 300 HP, throws orange ball for 50.
- GIANT: SON only after 1700m, very big gray humanoid, 777 HP, stomp 70.
- APACHE: SON only after 1700m, helicopter, 500 HP, flies, bullets/rockets.

SHOPS
- Main Shop every 1234m, Upgrade Shop beside it, Ally Shop every 1667m. (Shady Guy removed.)
- Safe zone: 9 meter radius around shop center blocks enemy spawning and enemy AI entry.
- Main Shop uses coins. Ranged/melee buy once. Misc can be bought 10 times.
- Upgrade Shop uses crystals: Fire 10, Lightning Chain 76, Enfeeble 100, Freeze 105, Slow 90, Ultracrit 125, plus Ammo +50/+150, Max HP +50 up to +500, Extra Dash once, Extra Revive twice.
- Ally Shop uses tokens and spawns friendly timed AI exactly matching each ally description.
