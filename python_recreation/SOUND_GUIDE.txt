roll.wav           Roll used

throw.wav          Misc thrown/deployed
explode.wav        Rocket, grenade, napalm, orbital, stomp blast
purchase.wav       Any shop purchase / Apple Pay style sound
coin.wav           Coin pickup
token.wav          Token pickup
crystal.wav        Crystal pickup
heal.wav           Medkit used
shield_on.wav      Shield or Disposable Shield activates
overdrive.wav      Overdrive starts
player_hurt.wav    Player takes damage
player_death.wav   Life lost
game_over.wav      Final death
